Welcome to Scoops & Smiles, the ultimate ice cream parlor! We serve a wide variety of frozen treats, including classic ice cream flavors, decadent sundaes, refreshing sorbet, and indulgent ice cream sandwiches. Our shop is a cozy and welcoming space, perfect for enjoying a sweet treat with friends or family.

At Scoops & Smiles, we believe in using only the best ingredients to make our frozen desserts. Our ice cream is made in-house using fresh, locally sourced dairy and the finest natural ingredients. We have a range of classic and creative flavors to choose from, including fan favorites like chocolate fudge brownie and strawberry cheesecake, as well as simpler flavors like vanilla bean and mint chip.

But it's not just our ice cream that sets us apart. Our sundaes are piled high with toppings like hot fudge, caramel sauce, whipped cream, and candy pieces, making them a decadent indulgence. We also offer ice cream sandwiches made with homemade cookies and your choice of ice cream flavor, creating the perfect combination of chewy and creamy. And for those looking for a dairy-free option, our sorbet is made from fresh fruits and is the perfect refreshing treat on a hot summer day.

At Scoops & Smiles, we strive to create a fun and welcoming atmosphere for all of our customers. We have plenty of indoor and outdoor seating options, making it the perfect place to catch up with friends, enjoy a date night, or bring the family for a sweet treat. We also offer special events, like birthday parties and ice cream socials, so you can celebrate your special occasion in style.So come on in and indulge in a frozen treat that is sure to put a smile on your face!
